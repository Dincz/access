Supplying products to leading brands in India for more than 32 Years

AVM APPLIANCES PVT. LTD. has come a long way since it was first established in September 1992. AVM started its journey by manufacturing sheet metal componenets and small assemblies for the fan industry. In the year 1998, we diversified into manufacturing of Air-Circulators and Light Duty exhaust fans for leading Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) in India. Today AVM has over 32 years of experience in the Fan Industry and we are the second largest Air-Circulator Fan manufacturer in India.

At AVM, 100% of production is done in-house. Our factory, consists of 24000 sq.ft, is located close to the city of Mumbai. We have latest quality testing equipment and have CB certification for export market & we are exporting to Middle East & African Country. We believe in exceeding the needs and expectations of our clients through timely delivery and continual improvement of our manufacturing systems and products while giving quality the upmost priority.
